Pyro C++ | A Novice Programmer's Baptism of Fire

A Brief Prologue

Computers are like fire.

They light up the world with information, sifting through a vast digital sea to deliver the right bits to the right ports.

They work with fiery speed, capable of doing billions of operations per second.

Like fire, they are powerful. They can beat the world's best chess players. They can see in pitch black via infrared. They can communicate without uttering a word. They can cripple entire countries if they aren't taken seriously.

And like fire, they also have a dependency... the oxygen that fuels flames is to the programmer that facilitates a computer's operation. If the programmer is incompetent then the computer's flame will die. To unlock the full power of these electronic behemoths requires discipline, devotion, and a deadset passion.

In this free online book, you will learn about the C++ programming language, one of the most popular programming languages. In fact, the operating system of the computer you're reading this on probably uses C++!

When used properly, C++ allows for faster computation than most other languages, and it gives you access into hardware components that are buried deep under layers and layers of abstractions. Indeed, C++ is one of the most powerful languages in the modern day, and plenty of jobs still ask for proficiency in it!

The aim of this book is to provide that proficiency, and hopefully spark a new interest in the incredible powers of computers.

Do not be overwhelmed by the complexity of the subject at hand, for Pyro C++ will be your shining torch of hope in the big, strange world of programming. So go forth and learn!
